# AI Assessment Test Pack

This pack simulates a hospital Document Control & C&P (Credentialing & Privileging) workflow for Agentic AI interview assessments.

## Folder Content
- documents.csv – Sample hospital documents with owners and expiry.
- hr_ipsg_list.csv – HR IPSG staff list with approval roles.
- email_templates.json – Email templates for expiry and review.
- whatsapp_flow.txt – Sample WhatsApp acknowledgment script.
- consultant_application.json – Sample specialist credential submission.
- policy_rules.json – Credentialing policy rules.

## Task Objectives
1. Automate document expiry detection, review routing, approvals, and staff acknowledgment.
2. Automate consultant credential verification and C&P approval routing.
3. Output dashboards, logs, and communications simulation.
